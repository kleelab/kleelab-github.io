Open position

We welcome all applicants interested in linking brains and computers. You will have the chance to work on the grand challenges of neuroscience using in-vivo neural activity recording, neural modulation, big data analysis, and new tools development.


Graduate Students:

If you are looking your a position as a Graduate student, please send me a state briefly what you are interested and attach a CV, including information about the classes and grades you had as an undergraduate. Please inquire and apply at: klee@dgist.ac.kr


Undergraduate Students:

If you are a DGIST student and interested in getting involved in our research, please get in touch us directly. If you are an undergraduate at other universities, you participate in the DGIST internship program and encourage applications through that program.
