Technologies

(한글)

Large-scale neural activity recording

Our customized silicon-based multi-channel electrode arrays can measure large-scale spiking activity with high temporal resolution in hundreds of individual neurons in localized microcircuits.

Simultaneous modulation and tracking of neural signals

Our customized opto-microprobe can manipulate and track real-time brain signals to reveal causal relationships in neural circuits and explore brain functions.

Wide-scope brain imaging

Our customized microscope provides real-time monitoring of brain signals over a wide spatial field of view (FOV) covering the multiple brain regions.
