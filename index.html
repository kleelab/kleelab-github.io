Neural Circuit Dynamics Research

Recording, Manipulation and Coding of brain signals


How electrical and chemical signals are coded in the brain? Neural Dynamics Lab addresses the fundamental principles underlying the functional brain. Our missions are to discover the neural dynamics from a single neuron to large-scale neural circuits in multiple timescales.

We employ the customized and integrated neural technologies with large-scale in-vivo electrophysiology, optogenetics, chemogenetics, fiber photometry, imaging, and pharmacological approaches. And, we are developing the next-generation neural tools to solve biological and technical issues and to answer scientific questions in neurobiological basis.
